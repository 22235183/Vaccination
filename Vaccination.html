Brief Introduction

  • As the pandemic has became more and more serious in everywhere,
  • scientists throughout the world have swiftly moved to develop safe and effective vaccines that will help reduce illness , hospitalizations, and deaths associated with COVID-19.s
  • There are two vaccine that have been developed. Which are called For CoronaVac (Sinovac) vaccine and Pfizer BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine.

CoronaVac (Sinovac) vaccine v.s. Pfizer BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine

CoronaVac (Sinovac) vaccine

  • developed by the Chinese company Sinovac Biotech
  • contains inactivated (killed) SARS-CoV-2 viruses
  • which causes the immune system (the body's natural defences) to produce antibodies and blood cells that work against the virus

Pfizer BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine

  • developed by the German biotechnology company BioNTech On August 23, 2021
  • by eliciting an immune response to the S antigen
  • The vaccine is used to reduce morbidity and mortality from COVID-19

How gorvenment encourage people to vaccinate

After the introduce of the vaccines, the response is not enthusiastic

Therefore, the gorvenment have introduced some schemes hope to raise people's response

Soft policies:

  1. Vaccination is free for everyone
  2. Welfares for those who have vaccinated
  3. It is convenient to book the vaccination. Link for online booking:Vaccination booking
  4. Advertisment are used to promote vaccination

Hard policies:

  • If citizen do not vaccinate, they cannot go to some public places e.g. restaurants
  • After the red code function in Leave Home Safe has appeared, people will get a red code if they haven't vaccinate enough dose
  • The policy forces citizen to vaccinate. If they do not, they cannot live normally. People can't even go to school or work.
  • For citizen who want to step out of their home to work or to entertain and students who need to go to school, they are forced to vaccinate.

Is the policy of vaccination effective to combate Covid-19?

Undoubtedly, the policy increased rate of vaccination.

This is the most effective way to prevent disease.

The principle of the vaccine is to inject the antigen or genetic material of the virus or bacteria into the human body, stimulate the immune system to produce antibodies,

so that when it is actually exposed to the pathogen, it can quickly produce an immune response and reduce the incidence of infection, severe disease and death

Getting vaccinated not only prevents you from getting sick, but also prevents you from spreading the disease to those around you.

When most people in the group are vaccinated, the spread of COVID-19 will be greatly reduced, so that everyone can be protected anytime and anywhere, and work and live normally
